What is Web Standards?

Web Standards are the technologies we use to build websites. It also ensures that the websites we build are usable and accessible to all,regardless of the device they use to access the internet.The standards specification covers over 90 topics such as Web Design Applications, Web Architecture, and Web Semantics.

The Central Orginaziation who is responsible for creating and maintaing web standards is the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)

It is important for webpages to comply with standards bacause your site will be easier to maintain, update, redesign and you can easily convert your web pages into other formats
